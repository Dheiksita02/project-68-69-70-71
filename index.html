Wisdom Wealth International School, one of the top ICSE schools in South Tamilnadu, was established with a vision to create leaders for creative age with personalized learning. It is a child-centric school with school architecture, academic curriculum and learning methodology designed for personalized learning. Child -Centric School

We firmly believe that every child has a potential to find his passion in a particular field and can pursue world-class careers in his passionate field. Every aspect of schooling has been devised keeping this vision of creating leadership qualities in each child and happiness of children in mind. Learning at WWIS is beyond the usual classroom and teaching and let children explore different facets of real life experiences.

We Understand Your Child

We don't restrict ourselves to any educational method. We follow the child. We redesign learning spaces, curriculum methods, and organization according to the needs of the child. We provide individual learning plans for each child and provide resources to suit passion of each child.
